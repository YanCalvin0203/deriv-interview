NovaPay Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect
NovaPay collects the following types of data from merchants and their customers:
- Personal identification information: name, email address, phone number, billing address.
- Payment information: card numbers (stored in tokenized form only), bank account details.
- Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, transaction history, and login timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Information
- To process payments and issue refunds.
- To detect and prevent fraud.
- To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
- To improve our services through aggregated analytics.
- To send transactional notifications and, where opted in, marketing communications.

3. Data Storage and Security
All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. NovaPay is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. Sensitive payment data is never stored directly on NovaPay servers — it is tokenized via our secure vault partner.

4. Data Sharing
NovaPay does not sell personal data. We share data only with:
- Payment processors and banking partners required to complete transactions.
- Regulatory bodies when required by law.
- Third-party analytics providers under strict data processing agreements.

5. Data Retention
Transaction records are retained for 7 years to comply with financial regulations. Account data is deleted within 90 days of account closure upon request.

6. User Rights
Merchants and their customers may request data access, correction, or deletion by contacting privacy@novapay.example.com. Requests are processed within 30 days.

7. Cookies
NovaPay uses essential cookies for session management and optional analytics cookies. Users can manage cookie preferences via the NovaPay dashboard settings.
