NovaPay Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Eligibility for Refunds
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of the original transaction date. Refunds are only available for transactions processed through the NovaPay platform.

2. Refund Process
To initiate a refund, merchants must log in to the NovaPay dashboard and navigate to the Transactions section. Select the transaction and click "Issue Refund." Refunds can be full or partial.

Alternatively, merchants can use the NovaPay API endpoint POST /v1/refunds with the transaction ID and refund amount in the request body.

3. Refund Timeline
- Credit/debit card refunds: 5–10 business days to appear on the customer's statement.
- Wallet refunds: Within 24 hours.
- Bank transfer refunds: 3–7 business days.

4. Non-Refundable Items
- Transaction fees charged by NovaPay are non-refundable.
- Refunds for digital goods or services are subject to merchant discretion.

5. Disputes and Chargebacks
If a customer disputes a charge directly with their bank (chargeback), NovaPay will freeze the disputed funds pending investigation. Merchants have 7 days to provide evidence to contest the chargeback. Uncontested chargebacks result in a $15 chargeback fee deducted from the merchant's account.

6. Contact
For refund-related questions, contact NovaPay support at support@novapay.example.com or call +1-800-NOVAPAY.
